Project Overview

For this homework project I aimed to analyze the reviews for the movie called The Fault in Our Stars. I chose this movie because I had mixed feelings after watching it and couldn't decide whether I liked it or not, so I wanted to know the thoughts of the public. To get the data, I used a package called imdbpie. As for the technique, I applied Sentiment analysis using the SentimentIntensityAnalyzer() function from nltk package, which uses Natural Language Processing to process the data. I also looked at the word frequency for the nouns and adjectives using another function from nltk.

Implementation

The first major component of my project is the retrieval of data. In order to get the reviews of The Fault in Our Stars, I had to get its IMDB ID from its IMDB url and used it as a reference to retrieve the data from the imdbpie package. In this project I only retrieved the movie title and the reviews in IMDB.

For the second major component, I had to prepare the data for analysis. This step included tokenization, cleaning the data, and identifying parts-of-speech tags. Tokenization refers to splitting the chunk of reviews into separate sentences and words. I needed this to access each sentence and word of each review. Next, I cleaned the data by removing stop words and punctuations. This allowed me to filter out words that are unnecessary. Stop words are words that don't contribute significant meaning to the text analysis (example: to, and, i, etc.) I got the list of stop words from the nltk package, and the list of punctuation marks from the string package. For the final step in data preparation, I identified the parts-of-speech tag for each word. This way, I was able to identify the nouns and adjectives in the reviews.

The final major component is data visualization and sentiment analysis. For the first one, I looked at the top 15 nouns in the review. This provides contextual information about the movie, allowing the user to get a sense of what the movie is about. For the second one, I checked the top 15 adjectives. This shows what the IMDB users think of the movie overall. Lastly, I performed a seentiment analysis for each of the sentences in the review section, to determine if they are either positive, negative, or neutral. For this step I used the SentimentIntensityAnalyzer() function from nltk package, which computes the sentiment scores for each tokenized sentence (positive, neutral, negative, compound).
